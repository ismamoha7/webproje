Our Baking Philosophy

At Sweet Crumbs, we believe that great baking takes patience. Our signature sourdough loaves undergo a slow, 24-hour natural fermentation process using wild starters, giving them an unforgettable texture and deep flavor profile.

We work exclusively with local millers to secure organic, unbleached grains, ensuring that every pastry, croissant, and custom cake we prepare is entirely free of artificial preservatives.
