Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our ingredients, ordering, and pick-up policies.

❓ Do you offer gluten-free bread options?

While we do bake specific flourless treats, all of our traditional breads are prepared in an open kitchen space that processes wheat flour. If you have a severe allergy or celiac disease, please be aware that cross-contamination is a possibility.

❓ How far in advance do I need to order a custom cake?

For small birthday or anniversary cakes, we require a minimum notice of 5 business days. For large-scale events like weddings or multi-tiered corporate gatherings, please submit your reservation request via our Custom Cakes form at least 2 weeks in advance.

❓ What is the best way to store your artisanal sourdough?

Because we do not use artificial preservatives, our bread is best enjoyed within 3 days of purchase. Keep your loaf cut-side down on a wooden cutting board or wrapped tightly in a clean cotton tea towel. Avoid refrigerating your bread as it causes the crumb to dry out rapidly.
