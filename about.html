Our Story & Heritage

Meet the hands, the hearts, and the history behind your favorite daily bakes.

From a Small Kitchen to the Neighborhood Deck Oven

Founded in 2018, Sweet Crumbs began as a passionate weekend project in a small family kitchen. Our founder, Jamal Mohamed, wanted to bring back the missing element in modern grocery store bread: time. What started as sharing a few sourdough loaves with close neighbors quickly turned into a bustling community staple.

We pride ourselves on using century-old European baking methods. Our main starter, lovingly nicknamed "Goldie," has been kept alive and fed daily for over eight years, giving our breads their unique, complex signature tang.
